Electric Coil Service is a leading, independently owned manufacturer of replacement electric coils — built one coil, and one customer, at a time.

Our 32,000 sq. ft. plant in Temple, PA is dedicated solely to making coils for your motor-repair needs. Our technicians have years of experience with all types and sizes of stator and armature coils, and every member of the team is satisfied only when we deliver the finest replacement coil available.

For more than seven decades we've built a reputation for excellence in coil manufacturing and customer service — guided by one standard: right first time… every time.
