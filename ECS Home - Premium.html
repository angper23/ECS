Our story

First in quality. First in service. Built one coil, and one customer, at a time — since 1951.

Electric Coil Service is an independently owned manufacturer of replacement electric coils in Temple, Pennsylvania. Our 32,000 sq. ft. plant is dedicated solely to coils for your motor-repair needs. Our technicians bring decades of hands-on experience to every stator and armature they wind. For more than seven decades, that singular focus has made ECS the trusted partner of motor-repair shops, elevator companies, and industrial maintenance teams across the country.
